03. Some Things I've Built

Ongoing Research

MRI Hepatic Fat Grading

Implementation and validation of a color mapping technique for grading hepatic steatosis in the pediatric population (n=30) using MATLAB. The study demonstrated that Signal Intensity Loss (SIL) calculation is a reliable, non-inferior alternative (r=0.77) to the commercial PDFF Gold Standard. Presented at the Chilean Congress of Radiology (CChR) 2025.

  • MRI Physics
  • MATLAB
  • Pediatrics
  • Liver Imaging

Published Research

Fundoscopy Phantom Model

Validation study conducted with 119 medical students using the Kyoto Kagaku M82 phantom. Results demonstrated a 162.8% improvement in macula and fovea identification skills after simulation training. Published in Revista Latinoamericana de Simulación Clínica (2024).
